ELSA Project

ELSA stands for Enregistrement des Lots pour la Sécurité Alimentaire, it's a simple ERP designed for little structures. With this systems you can add production recipe, tracing production taks, sensors to analyze the environment, launch alarms when the condition aren't respected. The system can export all data in a CSV file to get a complete traceability on the production. More information here
